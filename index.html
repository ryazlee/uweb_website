OUR CORE VALUES
Mentorship
UWEB strongly believes in the power of mentorship. We have 3 tiers of mentorship that provide our members with stepping stones into their intended career paths: high school, undergraduate, and graduate level mentorship.
Diversity
UWEB strives to create an inclusive and diverse environment open to people of all walks of life. We accept members regardless of their major and while we are "Undergraduate Women in Economics" we welcome students of all genders to participate in our events and activities.
Community
UWEB strives to build a dynamic and stable community for its members. We provide our members with a family to fall back on through our community building activities, mentorship, networking events, Monday night study sessions, and more. Join UWEB and find your family here at CAL.
